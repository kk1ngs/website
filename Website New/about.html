MY STORY.

"Life is a journey, not a destination." - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Having resided in the city of Hong Kong for 13 years, I've always been surrounded by high-rise buildings and skyscrapers. From time to time, I still reminisce about the urban landscape of Hong Kong and its uniqueness. Such fond memories of the city sparked my passion in Architecture and Structural Engineering.

As a first year Architectural Engineering student at the University of Waterloo, my dream is to pursue a career in this field and design stunning structures around the world.
